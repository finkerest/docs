## 🌱 Quick Start

1. **Log in with X (Twitter) or your wallet**  
2. **Fill in basics** → Name, symbol, logo, category  
3. **Set token options** → Supply, creator fee, first buy, burn %  
4. **Launch instantly** → Your coin is live and tradable  

---

## ⚡ Core Settings  

| Setting         | What It Means                       | Recommended |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------- | ----------- |
| **Supply**      | Total tokens created                | 1B          |
| **Creator Fee** | % of each trade that goes to you     | 1%          |
| **First Buy**   | How much SOL auto-buys at launch     | 0.5–2 SOL   |
| **Burn %**      | % of first buy that gets burned      | 25–50%      |

👉 Start with defaults. You can always adjust later.

## 🏷️ Categories  

Pick a category to help people find your coin:  

- **Meme** → For fun & viral tokens  
- **Art** → For creative projects  
- **Tech** → Utility coins (can enable share fees)  
- **Fundraiser** → Charity & causes (can enable share fees)

## 💰 Share Fees (Optional)  

For **Tech** and **Fundraiser** coins:  

- Send all trading fees to a chosen **X username**  
- Great for collabs, influencers, or supporting causes

- **Burn** → 25–50% creates healthy scarcity
